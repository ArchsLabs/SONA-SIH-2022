          UPON hearing the counsel the Court made the following
                             O R D E R
This petition filed under Article 32 of the Constitution of
India  principally  prays  that  the  respondents-authorities  be
directed  to  conduct  another  examination  for  the  petitioner  who
missed the opportunities afforded in terms of the judgment dated
03.11.2017  passed  by  this  Court  in  Orissa  Lift  Irrigation
Corporation Ltd. v. Rabi Sankar Patro & Others etc. etc., Civil
Appeal Nos.17869-17870 of 2017 etc., reported in (2018) 1 SCC 468.
Since  the  directions  issued  by  this  Court  were  absolutely
clear, no case is made out for exercise of our jurisdiction under
Article 32 of the Constitution of India.
