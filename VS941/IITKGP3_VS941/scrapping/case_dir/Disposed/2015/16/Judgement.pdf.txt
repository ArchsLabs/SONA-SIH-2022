O R D E R
Interlocutory Application No. 175857/2019  has
been  filed  by  the  petitioners  to  dispose  of  Writ
Petition No.7086/2015 pending in the High Court of
Delhi in terms of an order passed by this Court on
01.02.2019  in SLP (Civil) Nos.1125 of 2014 and 1127
of 2014.
This  application  is  filed  in  the  transfer
petition by which the applicants have sought transfer
of the aforementioned writ petition from the Delhi
High Court to this Court.
During the pendency of this transfer petition,
this Court, by an Order dated 01.02.2019, disposed of
matters  in  which  a  similar  point  arose  for
consideration. This Court directed that the cases of
the petitioners in SLP (Civil) Nos. 1125 of 2014 and

1127 of 2014 should be considered in accordance with
UGC Regulations of 2016.
The  transfer  petition  is  allowed.  Pending
application(s), if any, shall stand disposed of.
We  are  informed  by  the  learned  counsel
appearing  for  the  Union  of  India,  that  UGC
Regulations,  2016  have  been  replaced  by  UGC
Regulations, 2019 w.e.f. 20.02.2019.  Regulation 17
of  UGC  Regulations,  2019  is  in  pari  materia with
Regulation 23 of UGC Regulations, 2016.
Writ Petition No.7086/2015 is disposed of with
a direction to the respondents to consider the case
of the petitioners in terms of Regulation 17 of UGC
Regulations, 2019 within a period of four months from
today.  
We  are  further  informed  that  the  petitioners
had made the proposal way back in the year 2008.  The
petitioners are directed to submit a revised proposal
to  the  concerned  authority  within  a  period  of  two
