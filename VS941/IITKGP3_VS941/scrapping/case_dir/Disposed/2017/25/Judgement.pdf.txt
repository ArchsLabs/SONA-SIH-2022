          UPON hearing the counsel the Court made the following
                             O R D E R
 
1.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
2.
We are not inclined to interfere. The special leave
petition is dismissed.  Pending applications stand disposed
of.
3.
However,  we  request  the  High  Court  to  decide  the
matter within a period of six months.
4.
At the same time, it is stated that some Committee is
undertaking the fee fixation in accordance.  It is open to
submit the report of the Committee before the High Court.
5.
With  respect  to  fee  structure,  the  fees  which  is
realised as per impugned order, will hold good till such
time the High Court decides the Matter.
6.
All the questions are kept open to be agitated before
the High Court.
