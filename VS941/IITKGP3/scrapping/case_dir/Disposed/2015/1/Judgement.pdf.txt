          UPON hearing the counsel the Court made the following
                             O R D E R
No affidavit has been placed on record in pursuance of order
dated  10.02.2020.   However,  Mr.  G.  Prakash,  learned  Advocate
appearing for the State submits that Bachelor of Vocational Courses
have been sanctioned as Self-Financing Courses in Aided Colleges as
per  UGC  Guidelines  but  the  Government  Policy  is  that  No  Self-
Financing Courses will be sanctioned in Aided Colleges.
In view of the aforesaid statement, Mr. Haris Beeran, learned
Advocate for the petitioner seeks leave to withdraw the instant
petition.
The  Special  Leave  Petition  is,  accordingly,  disposed  of  as
withdrawn.
