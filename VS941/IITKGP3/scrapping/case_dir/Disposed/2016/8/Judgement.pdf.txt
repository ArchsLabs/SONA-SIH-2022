Leave granted.
2.
We have heard the learned counsels
for the parties.
3.
By  the  impugned  order  dated  19th
August, 2015 the writ petition filed by the
appellants  has  been  dismissed  on  a
technical ground, namely, due to failure on
the  part  of  the  appellants  to  remove
certain defects in the writ petition.
4.
The order of this Court dated 22th
July,  2016  issuing  notice  would  indicate
that the notice was issued subject to the
appellants  agreeing  to  comply  with  the
directions of the High Court and remove the
defects.

5.
Learned counsel for the appellants
submits that the appellant would remove the
defects as pointed by the High Court by its
order  dated  14th November,  2014  and  19th
August, 2015.  
6.
On the aforesaid statement made on
behalf of the appellants we set aside the
orders  of  the  High  Court  and  direct  the
appellants herein to remove the defects  as
pointed  by  the  High  Court  by  its  orders
dated  14th November,  2014  and  19th August,
2015  within  four  weeks  from  today.
Thereafter the High Court is requested to
hear  and  decide  the  writ  petition  on
merits. 
7.
The appeals are allowed subject to
the above.
