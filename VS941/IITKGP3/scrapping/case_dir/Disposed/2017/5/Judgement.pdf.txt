          UPON hearing the counsel the Court made the following
                             O R D E R
Learned  counsel  appearing  for  the  University  Grants
Commission(UGC) has produced copies of certain communications
which enclose letter dated 19.06.2017 addressed by Ministry
of  Human  Resource  Development  (Department  of  Higher
Education),  Government  of  India,  to  the  Secretary,  UGC,
pertaining to the financial support which is to be given to
Punjab University, Chandigarh, (Respondent No. 1 herein).  As

per  the  said  letter  dated  19.06.2017,  the  Ministry,  after
examining the report of the UGC, has committed the financial
assistance to the Punjab University in the sum of Rs.207.80
crores for 2017-2018.  It also mentions the manner in which
the said Grant shall be defrayed to the Punjab University.  
Mr. Prasoon, learned senior counsel appearing for the
Punjab University, on instructions, states that University is
satisfied with the aforesaid quantum of Grant and he further
states that it is expected of the UGC and the Government of
India to keep on giving necessary financial assistance.  We
expect the UGC to fulfil the aforesaid commitment.  
In view thereeof, this petition stands disposed of.  We
make it clear that in respect of the other issues which are
pending  before  the  High  Court,  the  High  Court  shall  be
entitled to proceed with the same.
