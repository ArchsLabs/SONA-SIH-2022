Mr. P.V. Dinesh, learned Advocate for the University stated
that in case of any difficulty, such as if any of student contracts
COVID and for that reason is unable to appear in the examination,
facilitation in the form of another attempt would be given. It is
further  stated  that  the  attempt  of  such  a  student  at  such
examination  will  be  treated  as  the  concerned  student’s  first
attempt.  
The statement is hereby taken on record. The University shall
ensure that the statement is given wide publicity on its website
and in the print media.
With the aforesaid observations, the Special Leave Petition is
disposed of.
