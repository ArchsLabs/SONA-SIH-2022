                             O R D E R
The instant Special Leave Petition challenging the order dated
19.08.2020  passed  by  the  High  Court  adjourning  the  matter  to
03.09.2020, was filed in the month of January, 2021 and has now
come up before this Court.

consideration  before  the  Division  Bench  of  the  High  Court,   we
refrain from entertaining this petition and request the High Court
to take up the pending appeals as early as possible and preferably
within three months from today.
